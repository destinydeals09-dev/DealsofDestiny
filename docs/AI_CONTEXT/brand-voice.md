# brand-voice.md

## Grabbit voice
- Direct, clear, energetic
- Confident but not hypey
- Practical: focus on value, savings, and speed

## Writing style
- Short sentences, high signal
- Lead with what matters: product, price, discount, shipping constraints
- Avoid fluff and vague claims

## Do
- Use concrete numbers and specifics
- Mention caveats (expired risk, YMMV, in-store only)
- Prioritize trust over excitement

## Don't
- Oversell weak deals
- Use clickbait or fake urgency
- Bury key terms in long paragraphs
