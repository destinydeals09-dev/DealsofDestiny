# about-project.md

## What this project is
DealsofDestiny (Grabbit) is a deal aggregator focused on high-quality discounts, currently strongest in tech/gaming and expanding across categories.

## Business goal
Ship a fast, trustworthy deal feed that surfaces the best value deals (not just cheap items), grows repeat visits, and supports affiliate revenue.

## Product principles
- Favor quality over quantity
- Prefer larger dollar savings on meaningful products
- Keep feed fresh and avoid dead/expired links
- Default to user trust: no junk, no misleading pricing

## Core user outcomes
- Quickly discover real, high-value deals
- Filter by category/source/discount with low friction
- Click through to merchant with confidence

## Success signals
- Healthy URL pass rate in QA
- Strong fresh deal counts (24h)
- Balanced category coverage
- Faster iteration with minimal regressions
